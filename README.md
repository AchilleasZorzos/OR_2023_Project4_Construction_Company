# Construction Company Project
Project for the Operational Research 2023-2024 class in NTUA Applied Mathematics and Physical Sciences.
Related to the field of Project Management and simulates the design and construction of a two-story house 
with a basement and roof, through a series of tasks. Each task requires a specific time
frame to be completed, which is determined through research conducted prior to the overall 
construction process of the project.
